Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Oncology Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and health information you share with us. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, email, and phone number.
  • Medical records and related documents you upload for review.
  • Information you share during sessions and correspondence.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide the education, navigation, and professional consulting services you request — including preparing reviews, conducting consultations, and communicating with you about your case.

How we protect it

Your records and your case stay private, secure, and protected. Files are handled confidentially and used only to prepare your review. We limit access to those who need it to deliver our services.

Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share it only as needed to provide our services, with your consent, or as required by law.
