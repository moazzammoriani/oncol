Patient & Family Navigation through
Second Opinions

Personalized, physician-led guidance for patients and families facing a new diagnosis, difficult treatment decisions, or care across multiple systems.

A cancer diagnosis can leave anyone feeling overwhelmed by complex information, limited appointment time, and uncertainty around critical decisions. Our navigation service is built to bring clarity and reassurance — so you can move forward with confidence.

How we help

  • Make sense of your diagnosis, staging, and pathology in plain language.
  • Review treatment options side by side — standard of care, trials, and alternatives.
  • Interpret imaging, lab results, and medical terminology.
  • Prepare focused questions ahead of your appointments.
  • Navigate insurance, referrals, and care pathways.
  • Understand supportive care, side-effect management, and quality of life.
